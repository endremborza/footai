Plans - Ideation

Goal: Use AI to anticipate (future outcomes) and explain (past events).

Motivation: Lots of attention on topic, lots of data, lots of opinions, lots of signal, lots of noise, frequent events, easy-to-measure outcomes and a complete market built around it

Current draft:

  • Weekly fixtures (10 leagues, next 8 days) + odds & 3 LLMsâ€™ predictions
  • Evaluation page comparing model performance

1. Anticipating (future)

  • Fixture list with predictions from multiple LLMs
    • Confidence tracking - consensus among LLMs and odds
    • Different input conditions
      • Feeding LLMs the odds
      • Asking them to Explain
      • Feeding each others explanations
      • Adding match preview from sports journalists
      • ...
  • (Lack of) conditional independence forecasting
  • Long-term forecasting
  • Tracking of changes in odds leading up to an event
  • Getting different kinds of predictions - pundits, votes of subreddits, etc

2. Explaining (past)

  • Evaluation metrics (accuracy, returns, risk aversion)
    • Both for individual LLMs and ensemble betting strategies
  • Ask LLMs for justification, based on data from the matches
  • Bias detection (big clubs, big leagues, home teams, unexpected outcomes, changed odds)
  • Compare LLM explanations with human analyst / journalist

3. Potential Spinoffs

  • Cross-sport generalization - basketball, tennis, NFL
  • Leak-proof LLM testing playground
    • Prompts, ensembles, explanations, risk taking can be tested
